Meet the Creator

SMW ROM hacks let me combine all of my passions into one. Seriously! Graphics, music, story, level design, and programming. I wanted to make video games ever since I was little. I'm incredibly thankful to smwcentral community, who has developed tools to make level creation so much easier. I also can't believe how nice everyone is in the SMW community. It's thanks to the players' feedback and support that turn my ideas into actual gameplay. I love all of you! Kaizo is the friends we meet along the way, as well as endless suffering and turmoil and... oh sorry I blacked out for a moment there. Anyway, I hope you have fun playing these hacks!

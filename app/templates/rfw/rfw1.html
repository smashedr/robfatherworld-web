Robfather World 1

Are you ready to dive into the world of Kaizo hacks, but you're intimidated by how hard they look? Robfather World is designed to be the second-half to Learn 2 Kaizo, focusing heavily on platforming instead of raw kaizo tricks. Robfather World allows you to execute tricks in a forgiving environment to give you the courage to move onto the first wave of kaizo hacks. After Robfather World, I recommend playing Quickie World 1, Quickie World 2, and Baby Kaizo World.
